Buyer Seller Messaging Guide

Step 1

Click the Seller Link

On the product page, scroll down past the 'Buy Now' button to where it says "Sold by". Tap on the seller's hyperlinked name.

Step 2

Visit the Seller Profile

You will be taken to the seller's profile page. Scroll down and tap the "Ask a question" button to begin your message.

Step 3

Amazon Support

Amazon's Messaging Assistant will open. Select "An item for sale".

Step 4

Click These Options

Select "Something else" and then "Other" to categorize your question. This allows you to ask your question later.

Step 5

Write Your Message!

A text box will then appear where you can type your message. For faster help, please include the product name or ASIN.
